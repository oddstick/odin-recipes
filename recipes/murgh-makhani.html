Murgh Makhani

Murgh makhani is a flavourful, richly spiced Indian dish made with a creamy tomato base and chicken as the protein. Traditionally, leftover tandoori chicken would be simmered in this sauce and served anew. A tandoor is a clay oven used for cooking.
